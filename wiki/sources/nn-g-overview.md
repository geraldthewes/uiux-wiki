# Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g) Overview

## Summary
Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g) is a leading user experience research and consulting firm founded by Jakob Nielsen and Don Norman. For over two decades, NN/g has been providing evidence-based insights through research, consulting, and educational resources to improve user experience design.

## Key Takeaways
- NN/g emphasizes research-based, objective findings over opinion or trends
- Their work is grounded in extensive usability testing, eye-tracking studies, and quantitative analysis
- Signature contributions include the 10 Usability Heuristics and Discount Usability Engineering
- Provides extensive guidance across web, mobile, desktop, and emerging platforms
- Offers diverse resource types: articles, study guides, reports, books, and training programs

## Origins/History
Founded by Jakob Nielsen and Don Norman, NN/g has been a pivotal force in establishing usability engineering as a discipline. Their work has helped shift the focus from aesthetic-driven design to evidence-based, user-centered approaches.

## Applications
NN/g resources are applied by UX professionals to:
- Validate design decisions with research-based evidence
- Implement proven usability principles and heuristics
- Conduct effective user research using established methodologies
- Stay current with evolving UX best practices and emerging trends
- Build organizational UX capabilities through training and certification
