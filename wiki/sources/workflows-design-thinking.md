# Workflows: Design Thinking
  
## Summary
Design thinking is a human-centered approach to innovation that draws from the designer's toolkit to integrate the needs of people, the possibilities of technology, and the requirements for business success.

## Key Takeaways
- Human-centered approach focusing on user needs and experiences
- Iterative process involving empathize, define, ideate, prototype, and test stages
- Encourages creative problem-solving and innovation
- Helps teams tackle complex problems by understanding users

## Origins/History
Design thinking was popularized by IDEO and Stanford's d.school, though its roots go back to earlier creativity techniques and problem-solving methods.

## Applications
- Product and service design
- Business innovation and strategy
- Solving complex, wicked problems
- Improving customer experiences
- Organizational change and transformation
