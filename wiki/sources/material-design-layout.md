# Material Design 3: layout
      
## Summary
Summary of Material Design 3 guidelines for layout.

## Key Takeaways
- Key principles and guidelines from Material Design 3 for layout
- Part of the Foundations (Design Systems) of Material Design 3
- Best practices for creating consistent and accessible interfaces

## Origins/History
Material Design 3 was introduced by Google in 2021 as the next evolution of Material Design, focusing on flexibility, personalization, and cross-platform consistency.

## Applications
- Implementing layout in Android, web, and desktop applications
- Creating consistent user experiences following Material Design principles
- Following platform-specific adaptations for different screen sizes and devices
