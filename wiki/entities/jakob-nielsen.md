# Jakob Nielsen

## Summary
Jakob Nielsen is a user advocate, web usability consultant, and co-founder of the Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g). Known as the "king of usability" by Internet Magazine, he has been instrumental in establishing usability engineering as a discipline through his research, writing, and evangelism.

## Key Takeaways
- Co-founder of Nielsen Norman Group with Don Norman
- Author of numerous influential books on usability and web design
- Creator of the 10 Usability Heuristics for interface design
- Pioneer of discount usability engineering methods
- Recipient of multiple lifetime achievement awards in UX
- Known for evidence-based, research-driven approach to usability

## Origins/History
Nielsen began his career in usability engineering at Bell Communications Research (Bellcore) where he developed usability methods. He co-founded NN/g in 1998 with Don Norman to advance the field of user experience through research and consulting.

## Applications
Nielsen's work is applied by UX professionals through:
- The 10 Usability Heuristics for interface evaluation
- Discount usability engineering techniques for resource-constrained projects
- Usability testing methodologies including the "test with 5 users" principle
- Heuristic evaluation processes for identifying design issues
- Evidence-based design decision-making frameworks
