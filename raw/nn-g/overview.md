# Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g) Overview

Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g) is a leading user experience research and consulting firm founded by Jakob Nielsen and Don Norman. For over two decades, NN/g has been providing evidence-based insights through research, consulting, and educational resources to improve user experience design.

## Core Philosophy
NN/g emphasizes research-based, objective findings over opinion or trends. Their work is grounded in extensive usability testing, eye-tracking studies, and quantitative analysis to provide reliable guidance for UX professionals.

## Key Resource Types

### Articles & Videos
Hundreds of evidence-driven articles covering:
- Usability principles and heuristics
- Human-computer interaction (HCI) fundamentals
- Design processes and methodologies
- Information architecture (IA)
- Psychology in UX
- Visual design principles
- Web and mobile-specific considerations
- Emerging topics like AI in UX

### Study Guides
Curated collections of articles in suggested reading order:
- **UX Basics** - Foundational concepts for beginners
- **Web UX** - Website-specific usability principles
- **Visual Design in UX** - Principles of effective interface aesthetics
- **Information Architecture** - Organizing and structuring content
- **Psychology and UX** - Cognitive principles underlying user behavior

### Reports & Books
In-depth research reports on specialized topics:
- Mobile usability
- E-commerce usability
- Intranet design
- UX careers and management
- Accessibility guidelines

### Training & Courses
- Live online workshops
- Self-paced courses
- UX certification programs
- Custom team training

## Signature Contributions

### 10 Usability Heuristics
Jakob Nielsen's foundational principles for interface design that remain relevant after 30+ years.

### Discount Usability Engineering
Methods for achieving high-quality UX with limited resources, including the famous "test with 5 users" principle.

### Usability Methodologies
Frameworks for conducting effective user research:
- Think-aloud protocols
- Heuristic evaluation
- Pluralistic walkthroughs
- Consistency inspection

## Platform Coverage
NN/g provides extensive guidance for:
- **Web**: Responsive design, accessibility, performance
- **Mobile**: iOS and Android specific considerations
- **Desktop**: Cross-platform application design
- **Emerging**: Voice interfaces, AR/VR, AI-driven experiences

## How to Use NN/g Resources
1. **Start with foundations**: Begin with UX Basics or Web UX study guides
2. **Focus on problems**: Identify specific challenges in your project
3. **Seek evidence**: Look for research-based solutions rather than opinions
4. **Apply iteratively**: Test assumptions with real users
5. **Stay current**: Follow emerging research while maintaining core principles
