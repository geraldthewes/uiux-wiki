# How to Create Mobile App Wireframes (in Just 8 Steps)
## Balsamiq Blog

**Summary**
This Balsamiq blog post provides a clear 8-step process for creating mobile app wireframes, with specific attention to mobile-specific best practices like touch targets, thumb-friendly design, and platform conventions.

**Key Takeaways**
- 8-step process: user priorities → lean flows → paper sketching → low-fidelity digital → real copy → responsive testing → iteration
- Mobile-specific best practices: 44×44 px minimum tap targets
- Thumb-friendly design: placing important controls in easy-to-reach areas
- Platform conventions: iOS tab bars vs. Android navigation patterns
- Simplifying for small screens: progressive disclosure and content prioritization
- Testing on actual devices and in real-world conditions

**Origins/History**
Balsamiq is a company known for its wireframing tools, particularly Balsamiq Mockups, which focuses on low-fidelity wireframing.

**Applications**
- Mobile app wireframing from concept to detailed low-fidelity designs
- Ensuring mobile usability through proper touch target sizing
- Designing for platform-specific conventions while maintaining consistency
- Creating wireframes that effectively communicate mobile app structure and flow
- Iterating on mobile designs based on user feedback and testing
