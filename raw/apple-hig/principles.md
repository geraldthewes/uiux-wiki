# Apple HIG Principles

Apple's Human Interface Guidelines are built on three core principles:

## Clarity
- Text is legible at every size
- Icons are precise and lucid
- Adornments are subtle and appropriate
- Focus is driven by functionality

## Deference
- Fluid motion helps people understand what changed
- Crisp, beautiful UI never overwhelms content
- Translucency and blur hint at more content without duplicating it

## Depth
- Distinct layers and realistic motion convey hierarchy and context
- Vibrant colors enhance understanding without overpowering content
- Realistic motion conveys a sense of liveliness

These principles guide the design of intuitive, beautiful, and engaging user interfaces across Apple platforms.
