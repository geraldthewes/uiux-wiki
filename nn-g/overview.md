# Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g) Overview

NN/g is a leading user experience research and consulting firm founded by Jakob Nielsen and Don Norman. Their website (nngroup.com) provides evidence-based articles, videos, and study guides on usability, human-computer interaction (HCI), design processes, and more.

- Psychology and UX

### Templates and Reports
- Free templates for various UX activities

### Coverage
- Extensive web and mobile design guidance

Start with the UX Basics or Web UX study guide, then explore specific topics based on your needs. The study guides curate articles in a suggested reading order.
